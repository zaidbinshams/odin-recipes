Tea

Simple beverage

Tea is a common beverage popular in all parts of India.

Ingredients

  • Tea leaves
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Water

Steps

  1. Pour milk equal to 3/4th the amount of tea you want to make in a saucer.
  2. Add water equal to the remaining 1/4th.
  3. Add some sugar as per your preference.
  4. Add tea leaves as per your preference.
  5. Let it all boil for a few minutes.
