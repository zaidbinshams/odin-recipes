Maggi

Simple snack

Maggi is a common snack popular in all parts of India.

Ingredients

  • Maggi packet
  • Water
  • Red chilli powder

Steps

  1. Empty the contents of the maggi packet inside a small pan.
  2. Pour water into the pan. If you want it soupy, add a bit more.
  3. Empty the contents of the small packet inside the maggi packet into the pan.
  4. Add some red chilli powder according to your preference/spice tolerance.
  5. Let it all cook for about 6-8 minutes on medium heat on a stove.
